Rubik's Cube Solver

Interactive 3D Rubik's Cube with solver functionality

Interactive Cube

Drag to rotate the cube. Click buttons below to scramble or solve.

How to use:

  • Drag the cube to rotate your view
  • Click "Scramble" to randomize the cube
  • Click "Solve" to generate a solution
  • Click moves in the solution to jump to that step
  • Click "Hint" for a suggested move

Custom Cube Builder

Select a color and click on the cube to paint it.
